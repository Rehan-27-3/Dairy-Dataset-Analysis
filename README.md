#Overview
An end-to-end data analytics project focused on optimizing supply chain logistics, inventory management, and financial profitability for major Indian dairy brands (e.g., Amul, Sudha, Warana, Dodla Dairy). This project processes complex variables unique to the dairy ecosystem—such as highly perishable product lifespans, variable storage conditions, and multi-tiered retail/wholesale channels—to deliver actionable business intelligence.

#Objectives
Optimize Inventory: Automate restocking thresholds to eliminate stockouts and minimize dairy product spoilage.

Maximize Channel Sales: Analyze Wholesale vs. Retail performance to identify key volume and margin drivers.

Reduce Perishability Loss: Align storage conditions with product shelf lives to cut down expiration penalties.

Evaluate Brand Profitability: Identify top-performing dairy brands and high-margin product lines to increase net profit.

Streamline Supply Chain: Map regional production origins against consumer demand locations to optimize cold-chain distribution.
